Blue LED - invention that changed tech forever
Nobel prize winning invention
1 min read

  • Let's start with the basics | Why is Blue LED so important?

    December , 2020

    To begin , without blue LEDs, the world wouldn't have backlit smartphones, TV and computer LCD screens, Blu-ray players, many forms of lighting and countless other technological marvels.


    LEDs didnt have much industrial untill the 1990s ,when the Blue Led was invented .

    It was the missing link in the colour spectrum, red green and blue together form white .

    This missing link paved way for the screens that we have today .

    It's applications trickle down to even the white LED used for lighting in homes.

    LEDS are more energy-efficient and have a longer life span than conventional incandescent lights .

    The spheres of life transformed due to this invention by Isamu Akasaki, Hiroshi Amano, and Shuji Nakamura .
    It's applications also are to water purification which earlier used mercury lamps . References
